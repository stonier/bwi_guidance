Instructions

  1. In these experiments, you'll be controlling a simulated person and will try and find a red ball located somewhere on the floor. Images of the simulated person and the ball are below.
  2. The ball you are trying to locate may be in an open area, where it is easy to see. Alternatively, it might be inside a room and not easily visible from the outside through the doorway.
  3. There will be some robots in the environment that will try and help you reach the destination. These robots are pictured below:
  4. The robots will always try and do their best to direct you along the correct path to the goal. However, there might not be enough robots available in an experiment to make the path to the goal easy to follow.
  5. When you approach a robot, an arrow will pop up pointing you in the right direction. If an arrow does not pop up, it means the robot is there performing some other task, and you should ignore the robot.
  6. Your goal will be to find the ball as quickly as possible. Once you find the ball, run into it to proceed to the next experiment. At the end of each experiment, your score for that level will be displayed and added to your total score
